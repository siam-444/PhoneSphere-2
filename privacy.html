Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024. Your privacy matters to us — this policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Information We Collect

PhoneSphere is a read-only reference tool. We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use our website. We may collect limited technical information automatically when you visit, including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type (mobile, desktop, tablet)
  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • Referring website URL

This information is collected in aggregate and cannot be used to identify you personally.

2. Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser localStorage to remember your phone comparison list between visits. This data is stored entirely on your own device and is never transmitted to our servers. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser's local data.

We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies.

3. How We Use Information

The limited technical data we collect is used solely to:

  • Understand which features and pages are most useful to visitors
  • Identify and fix technical problems
  • Improve the overall user experience

We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Content

Phone images on our site are sourced from publicly available product image CDNs (such as GSMArena). When your browser loads these images, a request is made directly to those third-party servers. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any third-party services if you have concerns.

We use Google Fonts to serve typography. Google may collect anonymized usage data as part of this service.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect the integrity of our website. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. As we do not collect personal information, there is minimal risk to users in the event of a security incident.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of PhoneSphere after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at our contact page.
