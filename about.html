About PhoneSphere

We're on a mission to make smartphone buying simple, transparent, and data-driven for everyone in Bangladesh.

Our Story

PhoneSphere is a small team of tech enthusiasts who were frustrated by the lack of a reliable, up-to-date smartphone comparison resource tailored specifically for the Bangladeshi market. Prices in BDT were often missing, specs were outdated, and side-by-side comparisons were impossible to find.

We set out to build something better — a clean, fast, and genuinely useful tool that helps buyers make informed decisions without confusion or information overload.

What We Do

PhoneSphere aggregates specification data and local BDT pricing for the most popular smartphones available in Bangladesh. Our platform lets you:

  • Browse the latest smartphones with full technical specifications
  • Search and filter by brand, price range, RAM, and more
  • Compare up to 3 phones side-by-side with highlighted best values
  • Discover budget options and flagship devices in one place

Our Values

Accuracy: We verify every specification and price before publishing. If something changes, we update it.

Simplicity: No paywalls, no sign-ups, no ads interrupting your research. Just clean, useful information.

Transparency: We clearly note when prices are approximate or may vary by retailer.

Data Sources

Specification data is sourced from official manufacturer websites, GSMArena, and authorized local retailers. Prices reflect the Bangladeshi market as of 2024 and may vary by retailer, region, and promotional period. PhoneSphere is not a retailer and does not facilitate purchases.
